Our Story

Celebrating 25 Years of Fashion

Established in 2001, Surabhi Textiles has been the heartbeat of fashion in Karuvarakundu, dressing generations of families with quality readymade garments and sarees.

Welcome to Surabhi Textiles, Karuvarakundu's premier clothing destination. We host a massive collection of wedding silks, designer sarees, trendy ladies' garments, men's formal and casual wear, and cute kidswear. Every garment in our showroom is hand-selected to guarantee unmatched fabric quality and designs.

Conveniently located right opposite the Karuvarakundu Bus Stand, we make shopping easy, accessible, and delightful for families in Malappuram. Step in to browse our collections and experience friendly customer service that makes you feel at home.
